# 3DBenchy Specifications

The 3DBenchy is a standard 3D printer calibration model. The dimensions are specified as follows:

## Overall Dimensions
- **Length**: 60.00 mm
- **Width**: 31.00 mm
- **Height**: 48.00 mm

## Component Dimensions
- **Hull Height**: 15.50 mm (at the lowest part of the deck)
- **Deck Length**: 60.00 mm (overall)
- **Deck Width**: 31.00 mm (overall)
- **Bridge/Cabin Height**: 32.50 mm (from bottom of hull to top of roof)
- **Chimney Height**: 11.00 mm (above the roof)
- **Chimney Outer Diameter**: 7.00 mm
- **Chimney Inner Diameter**: 3.00 mm
- **Stern Box Width**: 12.00 mm
- **Stern Box Length**: 7.00 mm
- **Stern Box Height**: 9.00 mm (from deck)

## Angles and Slopes
- **Bow Overhang**: 40 degrees from vertical
- **Roof Inclination**: 5.5 degrees
- **Stern inclination**: 15 degrees from vertical

## Features
- **Nameplate**: "3DBenchy" embossed on the stern.
- **Cargo Box**: Located on the deck at the stern.
- **Chimney**: Cylindrical feature on top of the cabin.
- **Cabin**: Main structure with windows.
- **Hull**: Boat-shaped base.
