# 3DBenchy Specifications

The 3DBenchy is a standard 3D printer calibration model.

## Key Dimensions
- **Total length:** 60.00 mm
- **Total width:** 31.00 mm
- **Total height:** 48.00 mm

## Features
- **Hull:** Complex curved surface.
- **Deck:** Flat surface with a small rim.
- **Cabin:** Rectangular structure with windows and a door.
- **Chimney:** Cylindrical extrusion on top of the cabin.
- **Bow:** 40° overhang.
- **Roof:** 5.5° inclination.

## STL Bounding Box
- X: 60.00 mm
- Y: 31.00 mm
- Z: 48.00 mm
