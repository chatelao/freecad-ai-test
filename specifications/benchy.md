# 3DBenchy Specifications

The 3DBenchy is a standard 3D print calibration model.

## Main Dimensions
- **Overall Length**: 60.00 mm
- **Overall Width**: 31.00 mm
- **Overall Height**: 48.00 mm

## Component Details
- **Hull**: 60.00 mm long, 31.00 mm wide.
- **Deck**: Elevated from the base.
- **Cabin**: Located on the deck.
- **Chimney**: On top of the cabin.
- **Bow Overhang**: 40°
- **Roof Inclination**: 5.5°

## Target Accuracy
- The model should be at least 99.5% accurate to these dimensions.
- Bounding box should be approximately [60, 31, 48] mm.
