# 3DBenchy Specifications

The 3DBenchy is a 3D model specifically designed for testing and benchmarking 3D printers.

## Dimensions
- **Overall length:** 60.00 mm
- **Overall width:** 31.00 mm
- **Overall height:** 48.00 mm

## Key Features
- **Bridge Roof:** The roof of the bridge has a 5.5° inclination.
- **Bow Overhang:** The bow has a 40° overhang.
- **Chimney:** Cylindrical with a hole.
- **Cargo Box:** Rectangular box at the stern.
