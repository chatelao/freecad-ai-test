# 3DBenchy Specifications

## Dimensions
- Overall Length: 60 mm
- Overall Width: 31 mm
- Overall Height: 48 mm

## Key Features
- Hull: 60 mm long, 31 mm wide.
- Bow Overhang: 40°.
- Bridge Roof Inclination: 5.5°.
- Chimney Diameter: 7 mm (outer), 3 mm (inner).
- Stern Nameplate: Recessed text.
- Cargo Box: 12 x 10.8 mm (internal).
