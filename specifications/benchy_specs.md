# 3DBenchy Specifications

The 3DBenchy is a standard 3D printing test model.

## Main Dimensions
- **Overall Length**: 60.00 mm
- **Overall Width**: 31.00 mm
- **Overall Height**: 48.00 mm

## Component Details
### Hull
- The hull has a complex curved shape.
- Bow overhang angle: 40°
- Stern overhang angle: ~15°

### Deck
- Sits inside the hull.
- Contains a small cargo box at the rear.

### Cabin
- Rectangular base with arched windows.
- Front window, side windows, and a rear door-like opening.
- Roof has a 5.5° inclination.

### Chimney
- Cylindrical, located on top of the cabin.
- Diameter: ~7 mm
- Height: ~11 mm
- Hollow inside.

## Verification Features
- Bridge over the deck (cabin roof).
- Tiny details like the nameplate on the stern.
- Circular holes (e.g., the chimney and the hawsepipe).
