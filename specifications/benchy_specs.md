# 3DBenchy Specifications

The 3DBenchy is a 3D model specifically designed for testing and benchmarking 3D printers.

## Dimensions

- **Total length:** 60 mm
- **Total width:** 31 mm
- **Total height:** 48 mm

## Key Features

1. **Hull:** Large smooth curved surfaces.
2. **Bow:** Overhanging curved surfaces (40°).
3. **Cylindrical Shapes:** Chimney and hawsehole.
4. **Flat Surfaces:** Deck and transom.
5. **Tiny Details:** Letters at the stern and on the bottom.
6. **Symmetry:** The model is symmetric along the longitudinal axis.

## Detailed Parts

- **Hull:** Length 60mm, Width 31mm.
- **Deck:** Height approx 15mm from bottom.
- **Cabin:** Located roughly in the middle.
- **Chimney:** Cylindrical, located on top of the cabin.
- **Hawsehole:** Small hole at the bow for the anchor chain.
